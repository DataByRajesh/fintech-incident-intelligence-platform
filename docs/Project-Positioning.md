# Project Positioning

## Why This Is A Career-Proof Project

FinTech companies depend on reliable systems, fast incident response, clear escalation paths, and accurate stakeholder communication. These needs remain relevant across application support, business analysis, systems analysis, production support, and technical consulting roles.

This MVP is career-proof because it demonstrates transferable skills:

- Understanding business impact from technical incidents
- Structuring ambiguous incident reports
- Prioritizing by customer, financial, SLA, system, and compliance impact
- Communicating clearly with technical and non-technical stakeholders
- Connecting intake, triage, tracking, and reporting into one workflow

## Why This Is Not A Jira Or ServiceNow Competitor

This MVP is not trying to replace enterprise ITSM platforms. Jira and ServiceNow are broad workflow systems with permissions, integrations, automation, approvals, audit trails, and enterprise governance.

This project is a focused portfolio MVP. Its purpose is to demonstrate:

- FinTech incident domain knowledge
- Triage and business impact thinking
- Front-end implementation skill
- Explainable rules and data flow
- Recruiter-friendly product storytelling

In a real organization, this concept could sit upstream of a ticketing system, support an analyst dashboard, or become a lightweight operational triage layer that sends structured data into Jira, ServiceNow, or another ITSM tool.

## FinTech Operational Resilience Thinking

Operational resilience in FinTech requires teams to understand:

- Which service is affected
- Whether customers are impacted
- Whether money movement or settlement is affected
- Whether SLA pressure exists
- Whether compliance or regulatory sensitivity is present
- Whether a workaround exists
- What stakeholders need to know now

The MVP reflects those concerns directly in the incident model and workflow. It does not simply capture a ticket title. It asks for business impact factors, scores the incident, assigns SLA status, and creates a summary that can be used in stakeholder updates.
